How to select parts:
(only one may be specified, for now.)

--match-all			All parts match.
--match-regex		Check regex against part number.
--match-regex-all	Check regex against part number, description, extended description (and hopefully notes as well).
--match-partno		Match part number.
--match-vendor		Match vendor.
--match-bin			Match primary bin location.


And then let's act on whatever parts matched;
multiple actions *shall* be run.
	
	--update-qty-db <filename>		If the on-hand quantity has changed, update the DB to match.
	--print	(text|json)				Print the parts as text or JSON.
